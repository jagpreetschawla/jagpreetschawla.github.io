I’m Jagpreet Singh Chawla, and this is my blog. With the recent developments in the field of CS, there are a lot of interesting things to learn and projects to work on. Although all necessary resources are easily available, they are scattered and finding and combining them is time-consuming. This is my attempt to help others by sharing my hard-earned knowledge and experience in a simple and intuitive method as I work on different projects.

About Me:

I completed my undergraduate in Information Systems from Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Goa. I am currently working as a server developer at Hike Pvt. Ltd. India, where I worked on several projects end to end. Most of my work experience includes developing server architectures and APIs, and deployment and maintenance of service running at scale. I worked mostly in Java and python. Other than work, I work on projects in various areas that interest me, especially related to Computer Vision, and Machine learning.
